A Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising (FIDM) graduate, designer Kijoo has worked in the fashion industry for over a decade. Her clean and contemporary designs bridge the gap between femininity and minimalist-dressing forthe fashion-conscious woman. As a senior designer, she spearheaded a team of creatives who researched, developed and turned over luxe collections that have been sold at the country’s top retail and department stores — both at a national and international scale. Now, Kijoo is applying her skills and creativity to create UI designs with user-centric design solutions.
